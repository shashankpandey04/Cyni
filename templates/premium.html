Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

Premium subscriptions are billed monthly at $4/month or annually at $40/year. You can cancel anytime and keep premium features until the end of your billing period.

Can I use premium features on multiple servers?

Each premium subscription covers one Discord server. For multiple servers, you'll need separate premium subscriptions for each.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

When you cancel, you'll keep premium features until the end of your current billing period, then automatically switch to the free plan.

Is there a free trial?

We don't currently offer free trials, but many premium features are coming soon and you can always start with our comprehensive free plan.
